    About Us

    • Ahub Trading Limited is based in Lusaka, Zambia with presence in China, Shandong Province. We are a specialised multidisciplined procurement and supply company delivering a complete solution to our customers. We specialise in the distribution of a wide variety of electrical equipment, office electronics, mechanical and automotive equipment, solar pumps, HDPE plumbing materials for the agricultural and mining industry and many more.
    • In a world of shrinking margins, Ahub offers a solution to your business. Our company was established with the objective to provide clients in Zambia with a platform to enable them conduct a hustle free business while importing from China, thereby saving them costs to maximize their profits. We are also an intermediary in the buying and selling transactions. We facilitate international trade between Zambia and China by making it possible for firms and individuals to import directly from the manufactures in China without the need for them to travel.
    • Our physical presence in China and vast network of suppliers backed with experience renders us key players in our client’s operations. We purchase goods on behalf of our clients, do quality inspection and ship them to Zambia. As a general trading company, we are pleased to provide a broad range of services, expanding them as needed to suit your particular needs.
